What is this?

* * *

ICCU's 2018 Events Week is a week of talks and activities about our Christian faith, based around the theme, "Home".

About the speaker

Stephen Foster

Preaching Pastor at Holy Trinity Brompton

Stephen Foster was born and bred in the majestic town of Luton. He read Law at St Anne’s College, Oxford, and Theology at Selwyn College, Cambridge, including research on the interrelation of justice and love. He worked as a Barrister for seven years, specialising in criminal defence – including serious fraud and large-scale drug importation. Stephen is now the Preaching Pastor at HTB, leading the Discipleship Team, the Bible in One Year app, and School of Preaching.
